FAQs

What is the purpose of this website?

The UML Class Diagram Builder is an online graphical tool used to aid in the design and creation of UML class diagrams. It attempts to bring creating UML class diagrams down to a level that any computer savvy user can perform.

Can I make as many UML diagrams as I want?

There is a limit of 25 UML Diagrams per project.

Can I make as many functions/variables as I want?

There is a limit of 10 variables and 10 functions per class.

Can I put any name for my title?

No, special characters other than “_” may be used. Also, class names cannot start with a number.

How do I view dependent classes?

To view dependent classes, press the small drop-down arrow on the parent class. A list of its dependent classes will appear below.
